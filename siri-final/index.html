The Story Behind
the Lens

Photography by Siri Naidu was born from a deep love for Indian culture, ceremony, and the quiet moments that make celebrations truly unforgettable. Based in Hyderabad, we have spent over a decade documenting Telugu and South Indian celebrations.

From sacred Vratham rituals to vibrant reception evenings, we approach every event with reverence, preparation, and genuine excitement.

Our Philosophy

We believe great photography is about presence — being in the right place, at the right moment, with the sensitivity to recognise when something beautiful is about to happen.
