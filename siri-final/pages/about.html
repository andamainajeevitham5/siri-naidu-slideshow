The Story Behind
the Lens

Photography by Siri Naidu was born from a deep love for Indian culture, ceremony, and the quiet moments that make celebrations truly unforgettable. Based in the heart of Hyderabad, we have spent over a decade documenting the full spectrum of Telugu and South Indian celebrations.

From the sacred Vratham rituals performed at dawn to the vibrant colours of a reception evening, we approach every event with reverence, preparation, and genuine excitement for what the day will bring.

Our Philosophy

We believe great photography is about presence — being in the right place, at the right moment, with the sensitivity to recognise when something beautiful is about to happen. We don't just take photos; we read the room, anticipate emotion, and capture truth.

Our editing style is clean, timeless, and carefully balanced — honouring the richness of traditional colours while ensuring every image feels fresh and modern.
